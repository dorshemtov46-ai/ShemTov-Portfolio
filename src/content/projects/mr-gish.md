Led the game design team of a non-profit bubble shooter called Mr. Gish aimed at diagnosing social anxiety among elementary school students.
Supervised the Game Design department, collaborating with a team of designers, developers and artists.
Conducted in-depth research on Level Design experiences and methodologies in the bubble shooter genre.
